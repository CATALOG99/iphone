    \n
  1. Put your AirPods in their charging case and close the lid.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  2. Wait 30 seconds.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  3. Open the lid of your charging case, and put your AirPods in your ears.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  4. Go to Settings > Bluetooth. Or go to Settings > [your AirPods].\n
      \n
    • If your AirPods appear there as connected, tap the More Info button  next to your AirPods, tap Forget This Device, then tap again to confirm.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • If your AirPods don’t appear there, continue to the next step. <\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Put your AirPods in their charging case, and keep the lid open. <\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Press and hold the setup button on the back of the case for about 15 seconds, until the status light on the front of the case flashes amber, then white.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Reconnect your AirPods: With your AirPods in their charging case and the lid open, place your AirPods close to your iPhone or iPad. Follow the steps on your device’s screen.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
